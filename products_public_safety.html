Case Study

ALTA SmartBalloons have worked in conjunction with a wide variety of public safety agencies across an assortment of mission profiles. We have experiences at the local, state, and federal levels in planned and direct response scenarios for a full range of incidents.

On October 10, 2015, ALTA became the first low altitude imagery vehicle to monitor a major sporting event as Florida State University hosted the University of Miami in NCAA Division 1 Men's Football. This marked a milestone in the public safety realm as ALTA captured information no other vehicle could, in ways no one else ever had.
