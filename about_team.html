Meet the Team

John Ciampa - Founder and Chief Executive Officer

John Ciampa founded Altametry in May 2011 with the goal of creating the world's first SmartBalloon using "Autonomous Lighter Than Air" (ALTA) technology. As CEO, he is responsible for overseeing all facets of company operations including research, development, production, distribution, and strategic partnerships.

Prior to founding ALTA, Mr. Ciampa served as the founder and CEO of Pictometry, the world's leader in oblique geo-referenced aerial imagery; which evolved into a company valued in excess of $700 million USD.

Mr. Ciampa is also the founder of Electronic Property Info Corp, and American Video Institute, a firm that commercialized projects from the precursor of MIT's Media Lab. Serving in that capacity, he pioneered collaboration with Russian projects and became the second American to be elected into the Russian "Tvochesrtva" academy.

He has also served on the faculties of New York University, Columbia University, Harvard Law School, and Rochester Institute of Technology; where he attained the rank of full professor thereafter publishing several books and articles.

Mr. Ciampa is the holder of seminal patents in computing, imaging, and geographic information, most notably Landisc; which received the Ford Foundation Public Technology Innovation Award presented by the Harvard University's John F. Kennedy School of Government.

Recently, Mr. Ciampa has been awarded three new patents for lighter than air low altitude image and information platforms that are the basis ofAltametry and ALTA SmartBalloon technology.

Mr. Ciampa holds a J.D. from Cornell University, a B.A. with magna cum laude honors in Political Science from Boston University, and has completed graduate work at both Harvard University, and the University of Michigan.

Dr. Bertrand Dano - Chief Scientist

Dr. Bertrand Dano joined Altametry (ALTA) in September 2011 as chief scientist. He oversees scientific research and product development for the entire range of ALTA SmartBalloons.

Dr. Dano has served in a robust progression of professional and academic appointments throughout his career. He presently serves on the research faculty for Florida International University (FIU).

Prior to ALTA and FIU, Dr. Dano served as adjunct faculty for the Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering program at the University of Miami from 2008 to 2013. He worked as a product engineer for Wind Power Systems of Boca Raton from 2011 to 2012, and as a technology scientist at the University of Washington (WA) form 2007 to 2008.

Dr. Dano is credited as an inventor on an assortment of U.S. patents including a vertical axis wind turbine, a discrete co-flow jet airfoil (DCFJ), and lighter-than-air-systems, methods, and kits for obtaining aerial images. Additionally, he is published scientific researcher who’s work has been instrumental in advancing airfoil technology development around the world.

Dr. Dano holds a PhD in Mechanical Engineering from Oregon State University (2006) with post-doctoral work in mechanical and aerospace engineering at the University of Miami from 2007 to 2010.

Prior to this, his academic preparation includes a M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Oregon State University (2000), a DEA in Aerodynamics and Thermal Sciences from Universite de Poitiers (France, 1997), and a B.S. in Physics from Université Blaise Pascal (France, 1995).

Nelson Melo - Director, Marine Research

Nelson Melo Director, Marine Research Nelson Melo joined Altametry (ALTA) in January 2014 as director, marine research. He is responsible for instrument development and remote sensing technologies across the range of ALTA SmartBalloon products.

Prior to joining ALTA, Mr. Melo served for 13 years at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) Atlantic Oceanographic and Meteorological Laboratory (AOML) as senior researcher in physical oceanography (regional shelf and oceanic research), marine remote sensing (remote sensors and aero-space digital images processing, applied to marine research) and marine instrumentation.

Previously, Mr. Melo worked as the director of research and environment; head of the research group in marine remote sensing at Institute of Oceanology (IDO), Cuba Hydrographic Institute (ICH), Geodesy and Cartography Institute (ICGC), Environment Agency, Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment (CITMA). Cuba. (1978-1999).

Mr. Melo is graduated at the University of Havana with master and PhD studies at the Russian Shirshov Institute of Oceanology (SIO) and the University of South Florida (USF).

Christopher Todd - Director, Marketing and Business Development

Christopher Todd joined Altametry (ALTA) in January 2014 as the director, marketing and business development. He is responsible for overseeing brand advertising, integrated marketing communications strategy, and product marketing program development with key partners.

Mr. Todd has provided expert commentary on digital marketing strategies to major media outlets including CNN, MSNBC, The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, USA Today, and many others.

Previously, Mr. Todd worked on a variety of start-up projects including developing a digital marketing strategy for PartyPoker.com (BWIN Party) that catapulted the online gaming platform to emerge as the world’s largest poker room within 24 months, and eventually become a publicly traded company (BPTY) on the London Stock Exchange.

Prior to that, Mr. Todd advised Fortune 500 companies on Internet advertising strategies while working as a technology analyst for New York based Jupiter Research. Before Jupiter, he worked at CBS Interactive as the senior manager of online advertising, and Entertainment Publications as an Internet marketing consultant launching Microsoft’s Sidewalk.com city guide project in the South Florida market.

Mr. Todd is presently working toward his M.S. in Integrated Marketing Communications from Northwestern University, Evanston, IL. He holds a B.S. in Journalism and Mass Communications with a double major in Advertising and Public Relations from Drake University, Des Moines, IA, as well as an executive certificate in digital marketing strategy from the Miami Ad School, Miami.

Mr. Todd is a licensed Florida Real Estate Broker, as well as a licensed U.S. Coast Guard Master of Vessels rated up to 100 gross tons.
